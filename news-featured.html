VorteX Blocked!

Posted in News · Vortex MH

Last thursday during around 5th hour, I got on Vortex to play crossy road (the best game on the website) and it was blocked. Obivously this is horrible for Blanchard, no games = no reason to go to school.

There are a few overlooked reasons why this happend.

#1. On the admin page, our url is "vortexgames". This is bad because it is openly saying that this is a gaming website. Take out competator, mathplayzone, for example. Their url has "math" in it, causing people to think it is a math website. That is why we are taking extra precautions to make sure that this does not happen again. We are changing the url to "blanchard.surge.sh" hopefully causing the district to think the name has something to do with our school.

#2. Only using one website. With our previous software, we had multiple websites to spread people across. This is proven to cause admin to overlook the website because of low traffic. We will also implement a multi-website structure, like before.
