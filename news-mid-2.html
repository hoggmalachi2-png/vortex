Minecraft Multiplayer Support

Posted in News · Vortex MH

Minecraft is one of our best games, and we're excited to announce that multiplayer support is now available! Well, sort of. From all of our tests we have concluded that Multiplayer Works on Lab Computers but not chromebooks. This is not for sure, so still feel free to test it out. But from all of our tests, we have not been able to play multiplayer on chromebooks. We will still work on a solution for chrombooks, but for now, enjoy multiplayer on your computers with decade old monitors!
